jsPlumb provides a way to "plumb" elements of a UI together. This page contains a little bit of everything to whet your appetite.

This demonstration uses jsPlumb 1.2.6-RC1, MooTools 1.3.2, and Drag.Move from MooTools More 1.3.2.1.
